Technology

Four Breakthroughs. One Platform.

ODEN R inherits the full ODEN architecture — every innovation, unlocked to its maximum configuration. Four patented breakthroughs working in concert to deliver manipulation capability that didn't exist before.

01

Independent Finger Splay

Each finger moves laterally as well as curling — just like a real hand. A novel MCP joint architecture enables abduction and adduction without the complexity of traditional robotic approaches.

02

Geometry-Embedded Tendon Routing

Tendon paths are encoded directly into the mechanical structure. No pulleys, no external guides. The geometry itself defines the biomechanics — fewer parts, fewer failure modes, higher fidelity.

03

Progressive DOF Unlocking

Start with baseline control, progressively unlock additional degrees of freedom as your algorithms achieve confidence thresholds. Deterministic manual control always overrides adaptive outputs.

04

Hot-Swap Modular Thumbs

Magnetically docked thumb modules with automatic topology detection. Swap opposition geometry in seconds — the system reconfigures instantly. No calibration, no downtime between experiments.
